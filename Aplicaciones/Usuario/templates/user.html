Account Deletion – Comexiger

Official account deletion request page for the Comexiger app.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your Comexiger account, send an email to

comexiger@gmail.com

No login is required to submit this request.

Please include the following information in your email:

  • Full name

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Personal information
  • Account credentials
  • Work performance records
  • Workday records

Data That May Be Retained

Some data may be temporarily retained for administrative or legal purposes before permanent deletion.

Retention period: up to 30 days from the date of your deletion request.
